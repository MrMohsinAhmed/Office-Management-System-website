OFFICE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (OMS)


The Office Management System, called as OMS in short, facilitates quick & easy accessing of data and information pertaining to Defence Standardisation Cell Avadi which are readily available in the system and updated from time to time.

This OMS enables correct & timely accessing of data and information, saving of effort & time in searching, independency on manpower in getting the required data & information, and self explanatory without the requirement of any special briefing to the newly joining officers and staff in the Cell.
